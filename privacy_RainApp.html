Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

RainApp is designed with privacy in mind. The app does not require an account. We only use the information necessary to provide weather forecasts and notifications.

Location

With your permission, RainApp accesses your device's location to display local weather forecasts and rain cloud tracking. Your location is used only to provide weather information and is never sold or shared for advertising purposes.

Weather Data

RainApp retrieves weather forecasts from trusted third-party weather providers based on your current or selected location.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, RainApp may send weather alerts such as tomorrow's forecast, your weekly outlook or significant weather changes. You can disable notifications at any time in the app or in iOS Settings.

Analytics

RainApp may collect anonymous usage analytics to help improve the app. This information does not identify you personally and is never used for advertising.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at email.
